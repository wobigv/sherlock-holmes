What are MysteryTrails?

MysteryTrails are all about following one of Sherlock Holmes' astonishing adventures and overcoming obstacles and solving puzzles while hiking in the beautiful landscape of Meiringen.

About
Trails

The trails are all in the surroundings of Meiringen and the Brienzersee. There are different levels of difficulty and lengths of paths. It’s most fun in a motivated group, but you can also walk most of the paths alone if you prefer so.
